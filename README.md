✝️ Glorify God: Digital Ministry

"Glorify God... with the word."

Welcome to the official repository for the Glorify God website. This project is designed as a peaceful, digital sanctuary for scripture study, meditation, and communal prayer.

🌿 About This Project

Glorify God is a single-page digital ministry created to help seekers and believers find a moment of peace in a busy digital world. It serves as a central hub for:

Scripture Study: Direct access to the Standard Works in both Spanish and English.

Audio Scriptures: Integrated headphone icons to guide users to audio versions of the word.

Scripture Music: A dedicated section for spiritual compositions designed to lift the soul.

Interactive Prayer Wall: A sacred space for visitors to leave their intentions and pray for one another.

Guided Meditation: A built-in "Quiet Time" timer to encourage daily reflection.

Missionary Connection: A direct pathway for those seeking deeper answers to connect with missionaries.
